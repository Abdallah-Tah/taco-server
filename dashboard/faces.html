How It Works
1. Add a Face

Click "Add Face", enter the person's name, and upload a clear photo. Use 1-5 photos per person for best results.

2. Face Detection

When the Blink doorbell detects motion, it captures images and runs face recognition on them.

3. Alexa Announcement

When a registered face is recognized, Alexa automatically announces "[Name] is at the door".

Tips for Best Results

Use clear, well-lit photos with the face visible. Frontal views work best. Add multiple angles for better matching.
